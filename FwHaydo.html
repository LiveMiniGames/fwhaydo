HAYDO'S HANGMAN
You privately set a secret word (masked entry, never shown on screen) and a timer. Chat guesses by typing single letters — every cycle, whichever unguessed letter was typed by the most different people gets locked in, not just whoever spammed it most. Classic hangman rules: 6 wrong guesses and it's over. Word only reveals when the round ends.

HAYDO'S INFECTION
Chat types !join during the lobby — one random player starts infected. Move with up/down/left/right (or !up/u/!u, etc) across a huge shared grid. Land on an infected player's square and you turn too, and can spread it further. Survivors win if anyone's left uninfected when the 5-minute timer runs out; if everyone turns first, the infection wins outright.
